Test Shooting

Nothing excites me like a new test shoot. Endless possibilities of exploring the artistic expression, portraying different emotion, discovering a new shoot inspiration mid process. Black and white photography is very close to my heart as I appreciate the timeless quality to it and how bare I feel while in front of the lens. As years went by I learned to love every flaw of mine and now I get super relaxed before a shoot knowing my strengths.
